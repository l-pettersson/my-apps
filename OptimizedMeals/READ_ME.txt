An app that minimizes the cost of your meals while still allowing you to reach your macros.

Set your macro goals in the main menu and add the foods you want to include in your diet through the food menu.
Click the '+' button beside each food item to set the constraints on the amount of food, all units are per 100g.

Once you have your food and goals set, click "Calculate" to view the minimum amount of food required to reach your goals.
